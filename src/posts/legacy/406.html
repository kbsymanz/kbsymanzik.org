We've had many people ask us what kind of school our kids go to.
We are so thankful for Faith Academy, its leadership and teachers.

"Since its founding in 1982, the goal of Faith's campus on Mindanao has been to provide a quality education for the children of missionaries. Faith Academy Inc. on Mindanao serves over 25 different mission agencies, as well as the business community. Approximately 130 students in grades K-12 represent more than 10 countries. Smaller classes allow for more personal interaction between teachers, students, and families.

Faith Academy, Mindanao, like its Manila counterpart, seeks to provide students with a quality, well-balanced, Christ-centered academic education....Faith Academy, Mindanao also has enrolled a limited number of international students."

Check out their website...and for any teachers, retired or active, consider coming over to provide quality Christian education to the MKs!!!! http://www.faith.edu.ph/mindanao/
