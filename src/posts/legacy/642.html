Well... I guess one way to deal with a hole in the road is to put an old tire around it, some rocks in the tire, a tree branch in it, mark it with a yellow plastic bag and put a saw-horse next to it.........

But, if it's a hole in the sidewalk, just make sure you are watching where you walk!
