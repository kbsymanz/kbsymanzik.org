This is a prayer I pray for my family and children....thought I'd share it with you.

To this end, I pray then always for my family; that You,
our God, may make each of us worthy of Your calling and
may fulfill every resolve for good and every work of faith by
Your power so that the name of our Lord Jesus Christ may be glorified in us and us in Him according to the grace of You,
our God and our Saviour, Jesus Christ our Lord.
(Based on 2 Thess 1:11-12)
