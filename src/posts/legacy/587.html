This morning I had a lump removed from my left breast. I will know the pathology report in about 10 days.
Pray for me to heal up from this minor surgery well.

A friend asked me if I was scared about the results.
No, I'm not.
I have a peace in my soul that comes from knowing that my Heavenly Father has everything in His control. I am resting in His hands.
