Bear, our dog, got out of the gate yesterday unbeknownest to us until a college-age young man came to inform us Bear was at the end of the street. (The gate had been left open by a propane delivery man on his way out.)

I ran down to the end of the street, knowing full well that Bear's physical size scares many around here.

Bear came to me when I called him. He really is a gentle dog, just large.

Across the street were college-age young women screaming because of Bear. And many of our Filipino neighbors were cautiously watching from their gates to make sure Bear was returned to our yard.
