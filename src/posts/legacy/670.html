As I watched our kids play with their Dad this recent vacation time at Eden I was thanking God for my husband and how he is willing to spend time with his kids.
I was also thanking God for the healing that has been occurring in our family, the strengthening of our family bond, and the way He is leading us.

Watching him interact with Mikayla, Kirsten, Ben and James I could see him meeting each of them where they are at....just like our Heavenly Father meets each of us where we are at!

1 John 3:1See what kind of love the Father has given to us,
that we should be called children of God; and so we are.

Children of the Heav'nly Father
Children of the heav'nly Father
Safely in His bosom gather;

God His own doth tend and nourish;
