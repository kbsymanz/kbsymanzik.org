I have treasured the words of his mouth more than my portion
of food. Job 23:12

I've been studying the book of Job and really find the above verse so true. His Word is more delightful than food. His Word is my lamp.
We really do not live by bread alone, but by His Word.

Matthew 4:4 ""Man shall not live by bread alone, but by every word that comes from the mouth of God.'"
