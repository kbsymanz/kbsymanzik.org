4 large bananas mashed
4 T baking powder
2 eggs
2 cups milk (more or less) OR 6 T milk powder and 2 c water
1/2 tsp cinnamon (more or less to taste)
1/4 tsp nutmeg (more or less to taste)
1/2 tsp salt
6 T sugar
3 cups flour
1 cup oatmeal

Mix well. Fry in oiled frying pan. Eat and enjoy! We love them!!!!

Serve with homemade syrup -
1 cup white sugar
1 cup brown sugar
1 cup water
boil for five minutes.
Add 1 tsp maple flavouring
Add 1/2 tsp vanilla
Add 1/2 tsp salt.
Boil one more minute. Then enjoy on pancakes or rice! YUM!!!!

(for different flavour add 1 tsp cinnamon instead of maple flavouring)
