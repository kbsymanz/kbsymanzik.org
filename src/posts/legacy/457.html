--- title: "If One Falls Down....." publishDate: 2009-09-15 modifyDate: 2009-09-15 legacy_AuthorID: bethsymanzik legacy_ID: 457 legacy_LastModID: bethsymanzik legacy_Category1: Christian-Faith legacy_Category2: Something-Different legacy_Status: 4 legacy_url_title: if-one-falls-down template: post.jade collection: posts --- As I watched Ben gently pushing Jerard around in his wheel chair this morning, this verse kept going through my thoughts "Two are better than one, because they have a good return for their work: If one falls down, his friend can help him up." Ecc. 4: 9, 10a

Ben is a good big brother to Jerard. He is a friend who helps him up.
Ben loves to make Jerard laugh, give him toys to play with, help him at meal times, etc.

A special needs child is a challenge, but we feel blessed with the opportunity and privilege of raising Jerard for God's glory, despite the challenges.
