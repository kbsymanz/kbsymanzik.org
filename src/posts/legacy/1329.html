Today, Feb. 20, we celebrate the 2 year anniversary of James' adoption.
He's been our son since he was a small baby, but in the eyes of the law he is now officially our son for 2 years which means we can reapply for his immigration visa to the USA!
Pray with us about that -- for it to go well and smoothly.
