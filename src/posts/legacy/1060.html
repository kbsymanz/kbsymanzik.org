It is not unusual to see an occasional mouse or cockroach in the grocery store while shopping (depending which store you shop at).
I figure the one that walked by my foot today slowly and unsteadily must be dying slowly from mouse poison!

I just kicked him out of the way!

Amazing, really, seven years ago I was terrified of roaches, rats and mice....today i pretty much view them as an annoyance but normal part of life here in the Philippines!

But word to the wise....wash the can lids prior to opening your canned goods!!!!
