"Brato lang" means "inexpensive" in Cebuano.

Today I purchased 50 roses for my birthday tomorrow.
It cost all of $5 US!!!
Checking the American Flower Deliver.com website, a dozen roses in the US costs $49 - $80.

I think my 50 roses for $5 qualifies for 'brato lang!'
