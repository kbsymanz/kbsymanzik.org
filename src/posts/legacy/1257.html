The other morning as I was in the kitchen making coffee at 4:45a I kept hearing a 'scritch-scritch' sound coming from under the dirty dishes in the kitchen sink. I figured it was a bug of some sort and went on with my morning routine. Later on I discovered it was one of these..............

a rhinoceros beetle

This is the second time I can recall finding one in our kitchen sink. Honestly, I prefer these to cockroaches any day (which I have found far more often in our sink but thankfully not to often!!!)!!!!
