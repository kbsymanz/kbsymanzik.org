Every year we celebrate Christmas here brings more and more melding of our western traditions with the fun traditions of our Filipino friends.
It doesn't seem so odd to be sweating and hot at the holiday time as it used to.
We truly enjoy the holiday season here and it feels more 'normal' to us.

But Mikayla and Kirsten were really missing going to the "White Christmas Party" that our pastor and his wife from our sending church in Michigan host every year.
So we hosted our own today.
We invited all the girls from the 6th and 9th grades to come to our house for tacos, gift exchange and watching "White Christmas."
The girls even wore funny hats which is traditional to do at the party hosted in Michigan!
The weather today was even 'cooler' (80's and lower humidity and a pleasant breeze) and we joked about the possibility of snow here!
We had fun!
