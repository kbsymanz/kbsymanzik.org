Recently Anne and Bob Owens from "Barnabas International" were here to encourage the SIL mission community here in Davao.

To find out more about 'Barnabas International' go to their website: http://www.barnabas.org/
"Welcome to Barnabas International!
Providing pastoral care to missionaries, MKs, global servants and their families."

One of the events we hosted was a women's supper at our house.

God has blessed us with a house able to hold many visitors.


Anne Owens

After a pot-luck dinner, Anne shared an encouraging message about how God orchestrates our lives for His glory and our good; and how encouraging it can be to others to share what God has done in our lives. After she spoke, there was a time for some of the women to share some of what God has done or is doing in their lives. It was a wonderful evening.

1 Thess. 5:11
Therefore encourage one another and build each other up, just as in fact you are doing.

Heb. 3:13
But encourage one another daily, as long as it is called Today,
