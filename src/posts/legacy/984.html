Ben and I think there is a sermon in these broken boards...

a message about how God can use the broken pieces of our lives to build His kingdom...

about how perseverance leads to the goal of His high calling

about how sometimes, often times in the struggle there is a wounding

like Jacob and his hip, like Paul with his thorn in the flesh
but in the end is triumph!

Philippians 3:13But one thing I do: forgetting what lies behind and straining forward to what lies ahead, 14 I press on toward the goal for the prize of the upward call of God in Christ Jesus.
