December 5th the girls played in a Christmas Band concert at the school's newly finished auditorium/performing arts center. It is a big blessing to have a nice building for the performances to be held in. The covered court could be very noisy when raining while the bands and choirs were trying to perform.

Of course we've been baking and decorating some Christmas cookies!

Last weekend we passed out Christmas goodie bags to very poor kids in a squatter area near by. Our friends, James and Jocy, run a weekly Bible Club for these kids. They provide an excellent ministry.
