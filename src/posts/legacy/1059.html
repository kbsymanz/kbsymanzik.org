I created a recipe that my family really enjoys so thought i'd share it with you all!

2 cups margarine
2 cups peanut butter
2 cups white sugar
3 cups brown sugar
4 eggs
4 tsp. vanilla
5 cups flour
2 tsp. baking soda dissolved in the vanilla or in 1 T water
1 tsp. salt
3 cups chocolate chips or m and m's
1 cup chopped nuts (optional)
1 cup oatmeal (optional)

mix all together. Spoon onto baking sheet. Flatten cookies with sugared bottom of a cup. Bake 10 - 14 minutes at 375 degrees.
Enjoy!
